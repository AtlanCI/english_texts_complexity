Mr. Smith is a farmer. He lives in the country near the forest. He has ten cows and makes very good cheese. Many people want to buy his famous cheese!
One sunny morning he goes for a walk with his dog, Spot. Suddenly Sport starts to do funny things and runs quickly into the forest. Mr. Smith follows him. What does he see? Sport shows him a little puppy dog sitting on the grass, under a big tree. The puppy was alone, hungry and cold.
Now the puppy lives in the farm with Spot and Mr. Smith, eats cheese every day and is very happy!
