John Watson is a journalist from London. He is married to Mary and they have three children - two boys, Peter and Albert, and a girl, Diana.
John is 47 years old. He is tall, broad shouldered, has short black hair and a mustache. Mary is 45. She has large glasses and she has long blond hair.
Peter is 15. He has short black hair, like his father. He is tall. His brother, Albert, has fair hair and is not very tall. He is 13 years old.
Their sister, Diana, is 17. She has beautiful long black hair. She is like any other teenager of her age. But every Friday she does something different. She cooks dinner for all her family. And everyone is happy!
The Watson family have a Labrador dog, Benji. They live in a small apartment in London.
